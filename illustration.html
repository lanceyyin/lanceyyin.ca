Celephais

This project is my interpretation of H.P. Lovecraft's short story "Celephais". It's about a man rejecting his mundane, pitiful reality in a desperate pursuit to live in the fantastical city he once visited in his dreams. I explored the contrast between the ethereal dream world and the cold, harsh reality through the use of colour and texture.
